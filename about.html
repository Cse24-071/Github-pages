About Maxwell Tech Store

Your trusted source for quality smartphones and tech accessories at affordable prices.

Our Story

Maxwell Tech Store was founded with a simple mission: to make high-quality technology accessible to everyone. Whether you're looking for certified pre-owned iPhones, brand-new Android devices, or reliable accessories, we've got you covered.

What started as a small passion project has grown into a trusted online tech retailer known for honest pricing, responsive service, and customer satisfaction. We're proud to serve tech lovers across Botswana and beyond.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Wide selection of smartphones and accessories
  • ✅ Certified and tested pre-owned devices
  • ✅ Competitive and transparent pricing
  • ✅ Fast, secure checkout and delivery options
  • ✅ Friendly customer support

Our Vision

To become Botswana’s go-to online destination for mobile technology, providing reliable devices that suit every budget and lifestyle. We aim to bridge the tech gap by offering affordable alternatives without compromising on quality.
